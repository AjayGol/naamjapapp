Privacy Policy

App: Naam Jap Daily
Effective date: February 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Naam Jap Daily ("we", "our", "us") handles information when you use the Naam Jap Daily mobile app (the "App").

Summary

Naam Jap Daily does not collect, store, share, or sell personal data.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal information or sensitive information from users.

  • No account creation
  • No server-side profile data
  • No analytics or advertising identifiers used by us
  • No location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data collected by us

How the App Stores Data

Your chanting progress, counters, preferences, and reminders are stored locally on your device using on-device storage (for example, AsyncStorage).

This local data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, notifications are scheduled on your device. We do not receive your notification content or schedule data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties because we do not collect personal data.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since app data is stored locally on your device, you control retention.

  • You can reset counters/history from within the app where supported.
  • You can delete all local app data by uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

Naam Jap Daily does not knowingly collect personal data from children or any users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
