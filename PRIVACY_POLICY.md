# Naam Jap Daily - Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** February 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Naam Jap Daily ("we", "our", "us") handles information when you use the Naam Jap Daily mobile app (the "App").

## Summary
Naam Jap Daily does **not collect, store, share, or sell personal data**.

## Data Collection
We do not collect personal information or sensitive information from users.

This includes:
- No account creation
- No server-side profile data
- No analytics or advertising identifiers used by us
- No location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data collected by us

## How the App Stores Data
- Chanting counts, progress, preferences, and reminder settings are stored locally on your device.
- Local storage is used only to make app features work on your device.
- This data is not sent to our servers.

## Notifications
- If you enable reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device.
- We do not receive your notification content or schedule.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties because we do not collect personal data.

## Data Retention and Deletion
- Data is kept on your device until you reset it or remove the app.
- You can reset data inside the app where supported.
- You can remove all local app data by uninstalling the app.

## Children's Privacy
Naam Jap Daily does not knowingly collect personal data from children or any users.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be published at the Privacy Policy URL with an updated effective date.

## Contact Us
For privacy questions, contact: **support@naamjap.app**
